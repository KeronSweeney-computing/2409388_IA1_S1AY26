AI Businesses of 2025 — Favourite Idea Report

Favourite Idea

Chosen idea: AI-powered (choose one from the blog list). Example: “AI Study Coach / Personalized Tutor for students.”

How it can apply in my world

As a Jamaican university student, I have to juggle several classes, homework, and due dates. In addition to creating practice problems for subjects like database design and web development, an AI study coach might assist me in planning my study schedule and decomposing difficult subjects into manageable sessions).

Why it would be useful

  • Benefit 1 (Saves time: Students don't have to wait for a tutor to provide them with practice questions and explanations.)
  • Benefit 2( Enhances performance: Rather than restating the student's prior knowledge, the AI concentrates on areas of weakness.)
  • Benefit 3( Promotes discipline: With reminders and well-thought-out programs, it assists pupils in developing regular study habits.)

Ethical Issues

  • Privacy: Student information, such as performance records and learning histories, must be protected.)
  • Bias: Without sufficient training, the AI system may not operate equally well for all students.)
  • Misinformation: The AI may occasionally give wrong explanations.)
  • Over-reliance: Students may rely too heavily on AI and stop thinking independently.)
  • Academic honesty: Some students may try to employ artificial intelligence to finish graded tasks unjustly.)
  • Data security: Personal information may be jeopardized if systems are not adequately secured.)

    • My Solutions to the Moral Concerns

    • Privacy: Only collect necessary data; encrypt; allow delete/export.
    • Bias: Test with diverse users; monitor outcomes; transparent reporting.
    • Safety: Clear limits, human oversight, and content controls.
